Hi there!  Welcome to the Thorne Inventory System!

When you're off this screen, here are some helpful keyboard shortcuts:
S: Sort
F: Find/Filter
M: Menu (this opens up the other options)
H: Help
D: Dump everything into from the retrieval chest into storage.
R: Recount the retrieval chest
Shift-R: Recount the entire inventory (this may take a few minutes, depending on the size of your structure)
Arrow keys: Move the selected item.
 - Left: Put one of the into storage.
 - Right: Take one item out of storage.
 - Shift-Left: Store everything from one slot.
 - Shift-Right: Retrieve up to one stack.
I: View Item Info
Enter: View Item Info
